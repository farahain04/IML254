My Education Journey

My Primary School

Sekolah Kebangsaan Bidor 2

2011-2016

This is where my journey began when I was in elementary school. I remember it very clearly, even though it happened a long time ago. Along with my friends, I rode my bike to school. So many memories were made, and of course, I accomplished a lot, like getting 6As on my UPSR. I also remember how much fun it was to play games and share snacks during break. Early on in elementary school, I learned the skills that would help me do well in school later on. It was the things I did and the people I met that made me who I am today.

My Secondary School

MRSM Sungai Besar

2017-2021

As a high school student, here is the place where my journey began. It was a fantastic adventure full with unforgettable encounters. Huffaz Al-Quran and other subjects not covered in other schools' curricula were both covered here. Many of my friends are from neighboring states, such as Johor and Sabah. It was a real challenge for me to adjust to this place, but I'm glad I did because it pushed me to develop my independence and maturity. Everything I experienced and learned at this high school will remain etched in my memory forever.

My University

Universiti Teknologi MARA Kedah

2022-2025

This is the start of my college year. I thought it would be hard at first because I would be living alone and far from my family, but now I love this place. The fact that we are free to wear whatever we want here. Campus life is lively, and there are lots of chances to learn and grow. I already have some great friends, and I can't wait for all the new things and difficulties that are going to happen. Finally, I feel like I'm getting used to this place. I'm excited to see how much I grow while I'm here at college.
